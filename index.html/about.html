Great Elite Academy is a government approved nursery and primary school established in 2013 and located at the peaceful town of Akuzor in Nkpor,Anambra state.It is suited in a serene,peaceful and conducive environment and equipped with modern state of of the art facilicities for proper learning as well as efficient mental development of your child of your child or ward

The school was established to provide a quality and sustable education in line with the objectives of Anambra state government education policy.Since its inception in 2013,Great Elite Academy has continued to remain focused and resilient in producing pupils who can easily cope with the challenges of a fast changing world in line with its vision and mission.This can be seen in all our pupils starting from our nursery kids who at early age can read,write, and spell accurately to our primary six pupils whose consistent performance in various competions,state,federal and private secondary schools examination has remained outstanding over the years with many acquiring full secondary education scholarship in top private and government schools in Nigeria.This has become a norm in our school.

Learning has been easy in Great Elite Academy with the provision of modern state of the art learning facilities,televisions in our nursery classes,a safe indoor and out play grounds,well decorated and ventilated classrooms,with efficient Teachers to carter for the whole being of your child.Also various academic clubs such as Mathematics and Science club,Spelling club,Drama club,Chess club and Debate club were created to help instill confidence,good communication skills and coperation amongst our pupils.

In Great Elite Academy,the spiritual and moral wellbeing of your child is not left out as we teach them the importance of drawing close to God and giving him a fervent service by being respectful and obedient.
